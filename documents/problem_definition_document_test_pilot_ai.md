# Problem Definition Document

## TestPilot AI – Autonomous Software Testing & Workflow System

---

# 1. Problem Statement

Software companies face delays in testing, high costs, and inefficient communication between development and QA teams before deployment.

Existing tools automate testing partially but do not address end-to-end workflow and coordination across the STLC lifecycle.

---

# 2. Problem Description

### Problem Context

Software development teams must test applications before deployment, but current processes are time-consuming and involve multiple manual steps.

### Customer Problem

- Manual or script-based testing requires time and skilled resources
- Communication between developers and testers is often inefficient
- Bugs are not properly tracked or prioritized, leading to delays
- Repeated back-and-forth during regression testing slows release cycles

### Typical Customers

- Software companies (startups to mid-scale)
- QA teams and development teams
- Product teams managing release cycles

### Why This is a Problem

Customers experience:

- Delayed product releases
- Increased testing costs
- Lack of visibility in testing progress

### Validation from Customer Discussions

- Customers confirmed that testing delays affect release timelines
- Teams highlighted communication gaps between Dev and QA
- Users showed interest in automation that reduces both testing effort and coordination overhead
- Majority indicated willingness to adopt a cost-effective solution that improves efficiency

### Willingness to Pay

Customers expressed interest in:

- Subscription-based pricing for automation tools
- Paying for solutions that reduce manpower and improve delivery speed

---

# 3. Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

### Industry

- Software development companies
- SaaS product companies

### Company Size

- Small to mid-scale organizations
- Teams with active development and QA processes

### Roles Involved

- QA Engineers / Testers
- Development Leads
- Project Managers

### Key Characteristics

- Focus on faster product delivery
- Looking to reduce testing cost and effort
- Open to adopting AI-based tools

### Pain Points

- High dependency on manual or scripted testing
- Communication gaps between teams
- Delays in bug fixing and regression testing
